How to Opt-In to OTTERalerts via Text Messages

By Hailey Gomez


Important Things to Know Before Opting In:
  • CSUMB employees and students automatically get emergency alert emails sent to their campus email whenever there is an emergency or a system test.
  • You need to have your cell phone number ready for text alerts.
  • OTTERalert notifications can be sent via text, email, desktop pop-ups, and outdoor speakers.
  • You can opt out of text alerts anytime by replying STOP to an OTTERalert text message.

Step 1: Visit the OTTERalerts Website

Navigate to the official OTTERalerts website by visiting this link. This page provides information about CSUMBâ€™s emergency alert system.

Reference: CSUMB OtterAlert System


Step 2: Access the OTTERalert Emergency Notifications page

On the OTTERalerts page, click the link that says "Add a cell phone number to start receiving emergency alerts via text." This will take you directly to the OTTERalert Emergency Notifications page on Oasis, or to the CSUMB account login page if you are not already logged in.

Reference: CSUMB OtterAlert System


Step 3: Log in to Your CSUMB Student Oasis Account

Log in to your CSUMB Student Oasis account using your username and password.

Reference: CSUMB Log In Page


Step 4: Click on the Mobile Phones Tab

Once logged in, you will be automatically taken to the OTTERalert Emergency Notifications setup page. Once there, and after accepting, click on the "Mobile Phones" tab on the "My Account" page to proceed.

Reference: CSUMB OtterAlert System - My Account Page


Step 5: Complete the Mobile Phone Opt-In Process

In the "Mobile Phones" section, add your phone number and follow the step-by-step process to complete the setup. You will need to enter your phone number, select your phone carrier, and input the confirmation number.

Reference: CSUMB OtterAlert System - My Account


Step 6: Save Your Phone Number

Verify that your phone number is correct and click "Save" to confirm. You will now receive emergency text alerts on your phone, and a confirmation text message will be sent to you.
